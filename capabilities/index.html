Overview

A complete banking ecosystem, owned by you

Most institutions run a patchwork of legacy cores, bolt-on vendors, and aging integrations. ACM consolidates the stack into one modern, brandable platform, so your team ships faster and your members and customers get a better experience.

Every capability below is white-label and client-owned. You control the brand, the data, and the roadmap. We bring the engineering, the compliance-ready architecture, and an Agile Speed Framework designed to move from discovery to production quickly, without the multi-year timelines legacy migrations usually demand.

The platform is built for regulated institutions from the ground up, with post-quantum cryptography, non-custodial options for digital assets, and real-time foreign exchange across global markets. Ecosystem partnerships with Hanzo.ai and Lux Network extend our reach in AI, data, and digital-asset infrastructure.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does the ACM Global Tech platform include?

It is a complete white-label banking ecosystem: core banking, deposits and lending, payments, mobile wallets, exchange and multi-currency FX, RWA tokenization, stablecoin settlement, treasury and liquidity, and card issuing. Every module is client-owned and brandable, so you control the brand, the data, and the roadmap.

Is the platform built for regulated institutions?

Yes. ACM is regulated-first and compliance-ready by design, with post-quantum cryptography and non-custodial options for digital assets. It is engineered for credit unions, community and mid-sized banks, and healthcare organizations that operate under real oversight.

How does ACM deliver faster than a typical core migration?

ACM uses an Agile Speed Framework focused on shipping working software quickly, moving from discovery to production without the multi-year timelines legacy migrations usually require. The platform also targets up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks as a platform value proposition.

Can we use only some capabilities and add others later?

Yes. Each capability runs on shared, modern infrastructure rather than as a disconnected point solution, so you can stand up what you need now, such as core banking or payments, and add FX, RWA tokenization, or stablecoin settlement over time. Schedule a discovery call to map a path.
