The Capability

Industry-leading exchange rates, owned by your institution

Most banks and credit unions rent FX from a third party and pass an opaque, marked-up rate to members and clients. ACM Global Tech builds the exchange layer into the platform you own and brand, so cross-border value moves at competitive rates with pricing your customers can actually see.

The result is a foreign-exchange experience that behaves like part of your core banking system because it is. Quotes, conversions, and settlement run on the same regulated-first infrastructure as deposits, payments, and treasury, with no separate vendor to integrate, reconcile, or explain to an examiner. Learn how it ships as a product in Exchange & FX.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What markets and currencies does ACM's FX capability cover?

ACM Global Tech delivers real-time multi-currency exchange and cross-border settlement across 200+ markets. Rates are built into your core banking stack, so quotes, conversions, and settlement run on the same infrastructure as deposits, payments, and treasury.

How are ACM's exchange rates more competitive than a third-party FX provider?

Because the exchange layer is part of the platform you own and brand, you control the spread instead of accepting an outside vendor's marked-up rate. Pricing is competitive and transparently displayed, so members and clients convert at the rate they see.

Is the FX capability white-label and client-owned?

Yes. Every screen, rate, and statement carries your brand, and the exchange capability is yours to price, position, and operate. It is delivered as part of ACM's complete white-label banking ecosystem.

Does ACM support stablecoin settlement for cross-border flows?

Yes. Eligible flows can settle over stablecoin rails for speed, with non-custodial options available where your operating model and risk appetite require them. All FX and settlement traffic is secured with post-quantum cryptography.
