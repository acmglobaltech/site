Thesis

More than capital. An unfair advantage.

Most funds write a check and wait. ACM Ventures invests in the categories we already build in, so founders get more than money: they get a working banking platform, a team that has shipped regulated systems, and a path into the markets they are trying to win.

We look for technical founders solving real problems for credit unions, community and mid-sized banks, and healthcare organizations. If your product makes regulated finance faster, cheaper, or safer, we want to talk early, when conviction and a few engineering decisions matter most.

  • Platform leverage: Portfolio companies can build on ACM's white-label core banking, payments, FX, and tokenization rails instead of starting from zero.
  • Engineering partnership: Access to a team experienced in core banking, settlement, and post-quantum cryptography, led by founder and CEO Michael Gladczuk and chief scientist Zach Kelling.
  • Ecosystem reach: Connections across the Hanzo.ai agentic-AI stack and Lux Finance settlement infrastructure that ACM is built on.
  • Regulated-first mindset: Guidance on building compliance-ready from day one, so examiner readiness is designed in, not bolted on.

How We Work

What a founder can expect

We are operators first. Our process is built to respect a founder's time and to be useful from the first conversation.

  • Direct conversations: You talk to people who have built and run regulated financial systems, not a screening committee.
  • A working platform, not a pitch: Where it fits, you can integrate ACM's banking, FX, treasury, and card-issuing capabilities to reach customers faster.
  • Long-term alignment: We invest in categories we are committed to, so our incentives stay pointed at your success well past the term sheet.
