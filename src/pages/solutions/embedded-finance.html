What it is

One platform, your brand, embedded everywhere

Embedded finance means delivering banking where your members and customers already are — at checkout, inside a partner portal, or within your own mobile experience — without sending them to a separate institution.

ACM provides the regulated-first core that sits underneath: deposits and lending, real-time payments, mobile wallets, multi-currency FX, card issuing, and stablecoin settlement. You expose only the pieces you need, under your own brand and your own control. The infrastructure is white-label and client-owned, so the relationship, the data, and the user experience stay with you.

This is the same ecosystem that powers ACM's core banking, treasury, and tokenization capabilities — assembled into clean, composable services so you can ship a financial product in weeks rather than rebuild a bank from scratch.

Integration & interoperability

Embed into the stack you already run

An embedded program lives or dies on how cleanly it connects to the systems your institution already operates. ACM exposes each capability as a versioned, documented service so engineering can ship without re-platforming.

REST APIs & SDKs

Provision accounts, initiate payments, issue cards, and read balances through idempotent REST endpoints, webhooks for event-driven flows, and SDKs your developers embed directly in app and partner code paths.

Core & ledger connectors

Reconcile embedded sub-ledgers against your system of record, posting transactions and end-of-day positions back to your existing core so finance keeps one authoritative book.

KYC/AML & card networks

Plug into your incumbent identity, sanctions, and KYC/AML providers, and route issuing and payment flows through established card-network and domestic-rail relationships rather than replacing them.

Sandbox & versioning

Build against a full sandbox with test data, then promote to production on stable, versioned contracts so a card or payment integration does not break when the platform evolves.

Deployment, residency & control

Run embedded finance where your regulators expect it

Where embedded balances, PII, and settlement records live is a board-level question. ACM offers deployment and administration models that risk, security, and procurement teams can evaluate against their own examination requirements.

  • Hosting models: deploy as managed cloud, hybrid, or private single-tenant so account, card, and ledger data for your programs can be isolated to your environment.
  • Data residency: pin storage and processing of member PII and transaction records to defined regions to meet residency obligations across the markets your program serves.
  • Roles & auditability: RBAC, SSO/SAML, and per-program scoping let you control which teams and partners touch which capabilities, with immutable audit logs across provisioning, payments, and card actions.
  • Security & compliance fit: post-quantum cryptography, segregation between programs, and reporting designed to support SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, and HIPAA requirements where healthcare flows are embedded.
  • Rollout via the Agile Speed Framework: stand up one capability in a controlled pilot, then expand to additional programs and partners on a predictable, examinable path.
