The problem

Models are only as good as the data beneath them.

Bank data lives in core systems, card processors, and a dozen point solutions that were never meant to talk. Analytics runs on stale extracts, every model team rebuilds the same features, and no one can prove where a number came from. AI on top of that foundation inherits all of it.

Silos

Data scattered everywhere

Core, cards, lending, and point solutions each hold a slice. No single view exists, so every question requires stitching systems by hand.

Latency

Analytics runs on stale data

Nightly batch extracts mean decisions are made on yesterday's picture. Fraud and risk need signal as it happens.

Duplication

Every team rebuilds features

AML, risk, and lending each engineer the same inputs in isolation, with no shared store and no agreement on definitions.

Governance

Lineage examiners can trust

Supervisors expect to know where data came from and who touched it. Ungoverned pipelines cannot answer that question.
