The Platform

One core instead of a stack of silos

Most institutions run a decades-old core surrounded by bolt-on systems for payments, cards, and digital channels. Every integration adds cost, fragility, and audit surface. ACM replaces that sprawl with a single, modern core where deposits, lending, payments, and digital banking share one data model and one source of truth.

The platform is API-first and cloud-native, so new products ship as configuration rather than as another vendor contract. It runs as your own branded environment, with non-custodial options where you need them, and it is designed to sit alongside your existing core during migration so you modernize on your timeline, not a forced cutover.

Capabilities

What the core delivers

A complete banking ecosystem, client-owned and brandable end to end.

Deposits & accounts

Real-time ledger for checking, savings, and certificates, with configurable products, fees, and interest engines.

Lending

Origination through servicing for consumer, mortgage, and business lending, with automated decisioning and faster cycle times.

Payments & cards

Card issuing plus modern payment rails and mobile wallets your members use under your brand.

Exchange & FX

Real-time multi-currency exchange across 200+ markets at industry-leading rates, built into the core.

Tokenization & settlement

Issue and settle real-world assets and stablecoin payments on institution-grade rails, with non-custodial options.

Treasury & liquidity

Unified position keeping and real-time liquidity, so funding decisions run on current data, not overnight batches.

Deployment

Cloud-native, on the hosting model your examiners accept

"Cloud-native" should not mean one-size-fits-all multi-tenant. The core is built on containerized, independently scalable services, then deployed in the topology your risk and regulatory posture requires.

Single-tenant by default

Run the ledger, payments, and tokenization services in an isolated environment dedicated to your institution, with no shared database tenancy across the deposit and lending books.

Hybrid and private

Keep the system of record in your VPC or private cloud while ACM operates the surrounding services, or run the full stack in a region you control for data-residency mandates.

Cost without lock-in

The elastic stack is engineered to target up to 95% lower infrastructure cost than legacy core systems, scaling with transaction volume rather than fixed mainframe capacity.

Integration

Coexists with the systems you cannot rip out

A core migration fails when it forces a single cutover. ACM's API-first design lets the new core run beside your existing ledger so deposits, lending, and payments move incrementally under the Agile Speed Framework.

  • Open APIs and SDKs: REST and event-streaming interfaces for accounts, postings, and card transactions, with idempotent webhooks for downstream reconciliation.
  • Connectors to what you run: integrates with incumbent core ledgers, card networks, ACH and wire rails, and your existing KYC, AML, and core-banking middleware.
  • Real-time reconciliation: shared posting events keep the legacy core and ACM ledger in agreement during dual-running, so finance closes against one set of balances.
  • Sandbox before production: a non-production environment mirrors your configuration for integration testing and examiner review ahead of any go-live.
