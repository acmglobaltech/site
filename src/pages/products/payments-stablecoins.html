Overview

One payments platform, two settlement worlds

Most institutions run card processing, ACH and wire, cross-border FX, and digital-asset settlement on separate, aging systems. ACM brings them onto a single, compliance-ready platform so payments move faster, reconcile cleaner, and cost less to operate.

Whether your members and customers pay by card, send an instant transfer, settle across borders, or move value in regulated stablecoins, the experience is real-time, traceable, and carries your brand end to end. The platform is client-owned, with non-custodial options where you want to keep control of keys and funds.

Capabilities

What the payments stack delivers

Production-ready payment and settlement rails you can brand, configure, and extend.

Card issuing & processing

Issue branded debit, credit, and prepaid cards with real-time authorization, controls, and spend visibility for cardholders.

Account-to-account & instant pay

ACH, wire, and real-time transfers with built-in validation, so funds and reconciliation stay in sync without batch lag.

Cross-border settlement

Multi-currency payouts and collections across 200+ markets, with competitive, transparent FX built into every transaction.

Stablecoin settlement

Move and settle value in regulated stablecoins on institution-grade rails, with on/off-ramp orchestration into fiat.

Mobile wallets

Branded, non-custodial wallet options that hold fiat and tokenized balances side by side for your end users.

Treasury & liquidity

Real-time visibility into float, balances, and settlement positions across fiat and stablecoin in one ledger.
