Overview

Move money across currencies and borders, on your brand

ACM Global Tech gives credit unions, community and mid-sized banks a complete exchange and foreign-exchange capability without building it from scratch. Quote, convert, and settle in multiple currencies from inside the same client-owned stack that runs your core, deposits, lending, and payments.

Members and account holders see your institution, your rates, and your experience. Behind the scenes, ACM handles the FX pricing, routing, and settlement plumbing so your team can offer cross-border services that previously sat out of reach for institutions your size.

The platform is regulated-first and compliance-ready by design, secured with post-quantum cryptography, and built to hold up under the scrutiny that serious banking executives apply.

Integration & Controls

Wire FX into the systems and controls you already run

The exchange and FX platform is built to sit inside an existing institution, not replace it. APIs and SDKs expose quoting, conversion, and settlement to your channels, while connectors bridge the core ledger, payment rails, and screening systems your examiners already know.

Rate sourcing and quoting APIs

Pull real-time pricing across 200+ markets, apply your own spreads and tiered rate sheets, and stream quotes into mobile, online banking, or your dealing desk through a documented REST and webhook API.

Core, ledger, and rail connectors

Post conversions and multi-currency balances back to your system of record, and route settlement over ACH, wire, correspondent, and stablecoin rails without rebuilding the integrations already wired to your core.

Embedded compliance screening

Sanctions and watchlist checks, KYC reuse, and transaction monitoring connect to your existing AML stack, so cross-border conversions clear the same controls as the rest of the institution.

FX desk administration

Role-based access, SSO, maker-checker on rate and limit changes, and per-currency dealing limits give risk and operations teams granular control over who can quote, convert, and settle.

  • Settlement-risk controls: position, counterparty, and intraday exposure limits are enforced inside the conversion flow, with breaches surfaced before a trade books.
  • Immutable audit trail: every quote, conversion, rate adjustment, and settlement event is logged with user, timestamp, and rate provenance, exportable for examiner and internal-audit review.
  • Data residency and tenancy: deploy in cloud, hybrid, or single-tenant configurations so FX and settlement data stays within the jurisdiction your regulator requires.
  • Designed to support SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI-DSS requirements: controls map to the frameworks bank and credit-union risk teams assess, and the platform is post-quantum secured for long-lived settlement records.

Because integration follows the Agile Speed Framework, branded FX can connect to your core and go live in phased, testable releases rather than a single high-risk cutover.
