Integration & Interoperability

Coexist with your incumbent core — no rip-and-replace

Few community banks can pause operations for a full core swap. ACM is built to run alongside your existing systems so you can modernize domain by domain while deposits, loans, and statements keep running.

Incumbent core & ledger

REST and event APIs plus prebuilt connectors let ACM sit beside an incumbent core during phased migration, with reconciled ledger posting so balances stay authoritative on both sides.

Payment rails & networks

Connect to ACH, wire, FedNow, and RTP, and to your existing debit and credit card networks — so new ACM products reach the rails your members already use on day one.

KYC/BSA & digital banking

SDKs and webhooks bridge your KYC, BSA/AML monitoring, and digital banking and statement vendors, keeping a single onboarding and transaction-monitoring path across old and new stacks.

Each connector is contract-bound and versioned, so a community or mid-sized bank can sequence cutover with its examiners and vendors rather than betting the institution on one weekend.

Administration & Auditability

Controls your board and examiners can verify

A community bank’s technology has to satisfy a board, internal audit, and FDIC, OCC, or state examiners. ACM puts the evidence they ask for inside the platform.

  • Role-based access & SSO: granular RBAC with segregation of duties and maker-checker on high-risk actions, federated to your identity provider via SSO and SAML/OIDC.
  • Tamper-evident audit logs: every configuration change, approval, and transaction is logged and exportable as examiner-ready evidence for safety-and-soundness and IT exams.
  • Compliance-ready by design: controls are built to support SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, and — for healthcare deposit and payment flows — HIPAA requirements.
  • Board-level reporting: access reviews, change history, and control attestations roll up into reports your risk committee and auditors can use directly.

The result is a platform a lean community-bank team can administer day to day while standing up to the scrutiny larger institutions take for granted.
