Who We Are

One banking ecosystem, owned by you

ACM Global Tech is a banking technology company, not a stack of point tools. We unify core banking, deposits and lending, payments, mobile wallets, exchange and multi-currency FX, real-world asset tokenization, stablecoin settlement, treasury and liquidity, and card issuing into a single platform that institutions brand and own.

Our approach is regulated-first and people-first. We design for compliance-readiness and operational simplicity from the start, then deliver through our Agile Speed Framework so modernization does not mean stitching together a dozen vendors or rebuilding your core banking platform from scratch. The result is infrastructure your team controls, with a value proposition of up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks.

What Sets Us Apart

Differentiators built for scrutiny

  • Industry-leading exchange rates: real-time FX across 200+ markets, with multi-currency and non-custodial options.
  • Post-quantum cryptography: NIST-aligned security engineered to protect long-lived financial records from “harvest now, decrypt later” risk.
  • Agile Speed Framework: a delivery model built for rapid production and iterative excellence across onshore, nearshore, and offshore teams.
  • White-label ownership: clients fully brand and own the platform — no patchwork of third-party point solutions.
  • Ecosystem partners: integrated with the Hanzo.ai agentic-AI stack and Lux Financial settlement infrastructure.
