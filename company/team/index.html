How we work

A team built for the way modern banks actually buy

Institutional buyers — from credit unions to mid-sized banks and healthcare organizations — expect more than a roadmap. They expect a partner who understands compliance, owns the engineering, and moves quickly without cutting corners.

  • Regulated-first by default: we design for institutions that operate under real scrutiny, building compliance-ready systems rather than retrofitting controls later.
  • Agile Speed Framework: a delivery model that compresses time-to-launch while keeping security, auditability, and governance intact.
  • Engineering ownership: the people defining the architecture are the same people accountable for it, from core banking to stablecoin settlement.
  • Client-owned outcomes: everything we build is white-label and brandable, so the institution — not ACM — owns the customer relationship and the brand.

Frequently asked questions

Who leads ACM Global Tech?

ACM Global Tech is led by Michael Gladczuk, Founder and CEO, and Zach Kelling, Chief Scientist. The company is based in Orlando and Jacksonville, Florida, and works alongside ecosystem partners Hanzo.ai and Lux Financial.

What is ACM Global Tech's leadership focused on?

The leadership team focuses on delivering a complete, client-owned banking ecosystem — core banking, deposits and lending, payments, mobile wallets, multi-currency FX, real-world asset tokenization, stablecoin settlement, treasury, and card issuing — built regulated-first for credit unions, community and mid-sized banks, and healthcare organizations.

What makes ACM Global Tech's approach different?

ACM pairs deep banking domain expertise with frontier engineering in post-quantum cryptography and AI. Its Agile Speed Framework compresses time-to-launch while keeping compliance, security, and governance intact, and everything is white-label so the institution owns its brand and customer relationship.
