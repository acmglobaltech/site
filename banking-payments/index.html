Banking & payments

Banking and payments, end to end.

The problem

A dozen vendors, one fragile seam.

Institutions stitch together separate providers for core, cards, payments, and FX. Every integration is a contract, a reconciliation headache, and a point of failure. The result is slow, costly, and brittle, and the customer feels every gap.

Core

Legacy and rigid

Decades-old core banking systems resist change, so launching a new product means quarters of work, not weeks.

Payments

Bolted-on processors

Acceptance, payouts, and ledgering live in a separate stack that never quite reconciles with the core.

Cards & FX

More vendors, more glue

Card issuance and cross-border FX each add their own integration, their own fees, and their own outages.

Ownership

Rented, not owned

The economics and the roadmap belong to the vendors. You carry the brand and the risk without the control.

What we deliver

One stack, gateway to backend.

A vertically integrated banking and payments platform that replaces the patchwork, so accounts, cards, payments, and FX share one ledger and one roadmap.

BaaS core

Accounts, deposits, lending, and treasury on a modern core, with the products you launch defined in software, not stuck in legacy.

Payment service provider

A Stripe-class PSP at the heart of the stack: acceptance, payouts, and ledgering, processing in and out on the same rails as the core.

Cards & wallets

Branded virtual and physical cards plus multi-currency wallets, issued and managed inside the platform you control.

FX & cross-border

Real-time multi-currency settlement across 200+ markets, so customers move money internationally without a separate FX vendor.

Tokenized & stablecoin rails

Stablecoin settlement and tokenized value alongside fiat, ready for the rails your customers are moving toward.

White-label & owned

You brand it and you own it. Every layer ships under your name, on your terms, with the economics and roadmap in your hands.
