FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is a white-label PSP?

A payment service provider you operate under your own brand. ACM runs the processing, orchestration, and settlement infrastructure; your institution owns the merchant relationship, pricing, and experience.

Which payment methods are supported?

Card acquiring, ACH, wire, and real-time rails, plus alternative and stablecoin settlement options, routed and orchestrated across providers for cost and reliability.

Is it built for regulated institutions?

Yes. It is designed to support PCI-DSS requirements with tokenization, layered fraud controls, and audit-friendly reporting, and it integrates with your existing compliance stack.
