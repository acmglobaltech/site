The Platform

One core instead of a stack of silos

Most institutions run a decades-old core surrounded by bolt-on systems for payments, cards, and digital channels. Every integration adds cost, fragility, and audit surface. ACM replaces that sprawl with a single, modern core where deposits, lending, payments, and digital banking share one data model and one source of truth.

The platform is API-first and cloud-native, so new products ship as configuration rather than as another vendor contract. It runs as your own branded environment, with non-custodial options where you need them, and it is designed to sit alongside your existing core during migration so you modernize on your timeline, not a forced cutover.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is a white-label core banking platform?

It is core banking infrastructure that runs under your institution's brand and ownership. ACM provides deposits, lending, payments, exchange, and tokenized settlement as one platform, while your members experience it entirely as your own product and your institution controls the data.

Can ACM's core run alongside our existing core during migration?

Yes. The platform is API-first and designed to sit beside your current core so you can modernize on your own timeline rather than through a single forced cutover. Skeleton deployments under our Agile Speed Framework get production capability live faster.

Is the platform built for regulated institutions?

Yes. The core is regulated-first and compliance-ready, with native audit trails and controls. It is secured with post-quantum cryptography to protect long-lived financial records and designed to be examiner-ready for the institutions we serve.

Does the core support tokenized assets and stablecoin settlement?

Yes. Real-world asset tokenization and stablecoin settlement are native rails in the platform, with non-custodial options. They run on the same institution-grade core as deposits, lending, and payments rather than as separate bolt-on systems.
