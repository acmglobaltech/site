Overview

One payments platform, two settlement worlds

Most institutions run card processing, ACH and wire, cross-border FX, and digital-asset settlement on separate, aging systems. ACM brings them onto a single, compliance-ready platform so payments move faster, reconcile cleaner, and cost less to operate.

Whether your members and customers pay by card, send an instant transfer, settle across borders, or move value in regulated stablecoins, the experience is real-time, traceable, and carries your brand end to end. The platform is client-owned, with non-custodial options where you want to keep control of keys and funds.

Built for regulators

Payments and settlement infrastructure engineered to stand up to examiner scrutiny from day one.

  • Regulated-first design: auditable transaction trails, controls, and reporting built for the institutions we serve, not bolted on later.
  • Post-quantum cryptography: long-lived payment and settlement records protected against "harvest now, decrypt later" exposure.
  • Non-custodial options: keep control of keys and funds where your risk and regulatory posture call for it.

  • Agile Speed Framework: our delivery model gets payment capabilities into production quickly, without compromising security review.

Integration & Interoperability

Settles into the rails you already run

The payments stack is built to sit alongside your existing core, card programs, and compliance tooling — not to replace them on day one. Connect once, then route fiat and stablecoin flows through the same orchestration layer.

Core & ledger connectors

REST and webhook APIs plus prebuilt connectors post entries to your system of record, so card, ACH, wire, and stablecoin settlement reconcile against one general ledger.

Card networks & bank rails

Integrations for card issuing and processing, ACH and wire, and real-time transfer schemes, with ISO 20022 messaging so cross-border and instant-payment flows carry structured remittance data.

KYC, sanctions & fraud

Plug in your existing identity, sanctions-screening, and transaction-monitoring providers, or use ACM's — screening runs inline on every transfer, including stablecoin on- and off-ramps.

Developer SDKs & sandbox

SDKs, a full sandbox, and idempotent payment APIs let your engineers test settlement flows end to end before a single live transaction moves.

Deployment & Controls

Deploy on your terms, control every ramp

Because stablecoin settlement touches custody, fiat conversion, and recordkeeping, hosting and operational control are evaluated as carefully as the rails themselves. ACM gives risk and IT teams concrete levers.

  • Hosting models: run the payments platform in ACM-managed cloud, hybrid, or private single-tenant, so settlement data and keys sit where your policy requires.
  • Data residency: pin transaction records, ledgers, and audit trails to chosen regions to meet local examiner and data-protection rules.
  • On/off-ramp governance: approval workflows, limits, and whitelists control how value moves between fiat and regulated stablecoins, with non-custodial options where you retain the keys.
  • Role-based access & SSO: RBAC, SSO, and dual-control approvals on high-value transfers, with immutable, post-quantum-protected audit logs across both settlement worlds.

Each model is rolled out through ACM's Agile Speed Framework, so you can stand up traditional rails first and add stablecoin settlement once controls clear internal review. See how it connects to treasury & liquidity and core banking.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is white-label stablecoin settlement?

It is payment and settlement infrastructure that lets your institution move value in regulated stablecoins on institution-grade rails, branded entirely as your own. ACM orchestrates on- and off-ramps into fiat and offers non-custodial options so you can keep control of keys and funds based on your risk and regulatory posture.

Can ACM's payments platform run traditional and tokenized rails together?

Yes. Card issuing, ACH, wire, real-time transfers, cross-border FX, and stablecoin settlement run on one compliance-ready platform. You can adopt tokenized settlement at your own pace alongside the bank and card payments you already operate, with a single ledger across fiat and stablecoin.

How does ACM keep payments secure and examiner-ready?

The platform is regulated-first, with auditable transaction trails, controls, and reporting built in. It uses post-quantum cryptography to protect long-lived payment records against harvest-now, decrypt-later attacks, and supports non-custodial options where your compliance posture requires them.

Which markets and currencies does cross-border settlement cover?

ACM supports multi-currency payouts and collections across 200+ markets, with competitive, transparent FX built into each transaction. Cross-border settlement pairs directly with ACM's exchange and FX product for consistent pricing across flows.
