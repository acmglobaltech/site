FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which industries does ACM Global Tech serve?

ACM focuses on three sectors: credit unions, community and mid-sized banks, and healthcare organizations. Each draws from the same white-label, regulated-first banking ecosystem, configured for its specific regulatory and operational needs.

Do we have to replace our existing core to work with ACM?

No. ACM is built to modernize incrementally. Most institutions begin with the capability under the most pressure — such as digital banking, payments, FX, or settlement — and expand across the ecosystem over time rather than forcing a rip-and-replace.

Is the platform white-label?

Yes. Every surface is client-owned and brandable, so members, customers, and patients experience your brand. The underlying core, payments, FX, tokenization, and settlement capabilities run beneath it.

What makes ACM's approach regulated-first?

ACM designs for regulated institutions from the start — with compliance-ready architecture, post-quantum cryptography, and non-custodial options where appropriate — so security and oversight are foundational rather than bolted on.
