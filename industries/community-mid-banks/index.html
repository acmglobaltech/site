The Challenge

Legacy cores hold community banks back

Aging core systems, rigid vendor contracts, and slow release cycles make it hard to launch products, control cost, and meet rising customer expectations — all while larger banks set the pace.

Community and mid-sized banks know their markets better than anyone. What they often lack is technology that moves as fast as their ambitions. ACM closes that gap with a modern platform that is yours to brand and operate, not a black box you rent.

  • Own your stack: client-owned, brandable banking infrastructure instead of a locked vendor relationship.
  • Lower the cost base: ACM targets up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks as a platform value proposition.
  • Ship faster: our Agile Speed Framework compresses the path from idea to production.
  • Stay compliant: built for regulated institutions, with controls and audit-readiness assumed from day one.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ACM's banking platform suitable for community and mid-sized banks, not just large institutions?

Yes. ACM Global Tech is built specifically for credit unions, community banks, and mid-sized banks. The platform is white-label and client-owned, so smaller institutions get megabank-grade capabilities while keeping their brand and independence, and can modernize one domain at a time rather than all at once.

Do we have to replace our entire core at once?

No. You can start with the highest-impact area and expand. Many banks begin with core and cloud modernization or a single new product on the ACM banking core, then add payments, FX, wallets, and tokenized settlement as their strategy progresses.

How does ACM address security and regulatory requirements?

ACM is regulated-first by design, with compliance-ready controls, segregation of duties, and audit trails built into the platform, plus post-quantum cryptography and non-custodial settlement options. The architecture is built for regulated institutions and the scrutiny their examiners and boards apply.

Can the platform reduce our technology cost?

Reducing the infrastructure cost base is a core value proposition. ACM targets up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks by replacing rigid, aging systems with a modern, cloud-native platform you own and operate.
