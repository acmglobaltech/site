The challenge

Treasury runs on yesterday's data and tomorrow's deadlines

Most treasury functions still stitch together bank portals, spreadsheets, and batch files. Cash position is a day late, FX is opaque, and cross-border payments settle on schedules set by correspondent banks rather than by the business.

For corporate treasurers, finance leaders at community and mid-sized banks, and treasury operations inside healthcare organizations, that lag is not a back-office inconvenience — it is working capital trapped in transit, hedging done on stale numbers, and counterparty risk that nobody can see until reconciliation. ACM exists to close that gap with infrastructure designed for regulated institutions from the first line of code.

One platform across the treasury lifecycle

Cash visibility, liquidity, FX, and settlement on a single white-label stack you own and brand — not a patchwork of point solutions.

Real-time cash visibility

Liquidity & cash management

Forecasting, pooling, and sweeps that move idle balances to where they are needed, with controls and audit trails built for regulated environments.

Multi-currency FX

Real-time exchange across 200+ markets with competitive, transparent rates built into the stack — no hidden spread between you and the quote.

Cross-border settlement

Move value internationally on modern rails, with stablecoin settlement and non-custodial options where they fit your risk and compliance posture.

RWA tokenization

Tokenize treasuries and other real-world assets to mobilize collateral and shorten settlement, with a clear, auditable record of ownership.

Risk & controls

Approvals, segregation of duties, and counterparty limits enforced in the platform, with post-quantum cryptography protecting data and instructions.
