The problem

Administrative friction is a financial problem

A large share of every healthcare dollar is consumed before it ever touches care. Reconciliation between providers, payers, and patients is still paper-shaped, and the float trapped in that process strains working capital across the system.

ACM treats this as an infrastructure problem, not a workflow add-on. By moving payer-provider settlement onto programmable, real-time rails, we compress the time between service rendered and cash received — and remove the manual touchpoints that drive cost and error.

What we deliver

Healthcare finance, on rails you own

A single white-label stack for payments, settlement, and liquidity — tuned to the way care organizations actually move money.

Revenue-cycle liquidity

Shorten the gap between care delivered and cash collected. Programmable settlement and treasury tooling free up working capital that would otherwise sit in float.

Tokenized payer settlement

Move provider-payer settlement onto real-time, auditable rails. Each transaction carries its own remittance data, so reconciliation is built in rather than bolted on.

Claims & administrative automation

Reduce manual handling across the claim and payment lifecycle with data-first automation, cutting the rework and exception handling that drives administrative cost.

Patient payments & wallets

Brandable mobile wallets and payment experiences for members and patients — flexible, transparent, and owned by your institution.

Stablecoin & cross-border settlement

Post-quantum security

Long-lived medical and financial records are a "harvest now, decrypt later" target. ACM builds in NIST-aligned post-quantum cryptography from day one.

Built for scrutiny

Regulated-first, examiner-ready

Healthcare and financial oversight are unforgiving. ACM is engineered for institutions that have to answer to both.

  • Compliance-ready architecture: auditable transaction trails, role-based controls, and data handling designed for regulated environments — not retrofitted onto a consumer app.
  • Client-owned and brandable: the full stack ships under your brand, with non-custodial options where appropriate, so the relationship and the data stay yours.
  • Agile Speed Framework: our delivery model moves capability into production quickly without sacrificing the rigor regulated institutions require.
  • Ecosystem depth: backed by the Hanzo.ai agentic-AI stack and Lux Finance settlement infrastructure, plus a dedicated healthcare delivery team.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does ACM Global Tech provide for healthcare organizations?

ACM delivers white-label banking technology for hospitals, payers, and health systems: revenue-cycle liquidity, tokenized payer settlement, claims and administrative automation, patient payments and wallets, and optional stablecoin and cross-border settlement. The full stack is client-owned and brandable.

How does ACM help reduce healthcare administrative cost?

By moving payer-provider settlement onto real-time, auditable rails and applying data-first automation across the claim and payment lifecycle, ACM removes manual reconciliation touchpoints and shortens the time from care delivered to cash received. JAMA and Health Affairs estimate roughly $570B a year in US healthcare administrative waste, the cost these rails are built to help reduce.

Is the platform secure and built for regulated institutions?

Yes. ACM is regulated-first and compliance-ready, with auditable transaction trails, role-based controls, and NIST-aligned post-quantum cryptography built in from day one to protect long-lived medical and financial records.

Can healthcare payments and settlement run under our own brand?

Yes. ACM is a complete white-label ecosystem. Payments, wallets, settlement, and treasury tooling ship under your brand, with non-custodial options where appropriate, so the member and patient relationship and the underlying data remain yours.
