The problem

Administrative friction is a financial problem

A large share of every healthcare dollar is consumed before it ever touches care. Reconciliation between providers, payers, and patients is still paper-shaped, and the float trapped in that process strains working capital across the system.

ACM treats this as an infrastructure problem, not a workflow add-on. By moving payer-provider settlement onto programmable, real-time rails, we compress the time between service rendered and cash received — and remove the manual touchpoints that drive cost and error.

Integration & interoperability

It plugs into the stack you already run

Hospitals and payers do not rip out an EHR or a clearinghouse to add a settlement layer. ACM connects to the systems already moving your claims and cash, so tokenized settlement and revenue-cycle liquidity reach production without a forklift migration.

EDI & clearinghouse-native

Ingest and emit standard X12 005010 transactions — 837 claims, 835 remittance, 270/271 eligibility — so adjudicated payments auto-post and reconcile against the rails ACM settles on.

EHR & revenue-cycle connectors

APIs and SDKs link patient billing, payer contracts, and your existing ledger, mapping each settlement event back to the encounter and claim it belongs to.

Payer & bank rails

Connect existing payer relationships, ACH and card networks, and treasury accounts alongside optional stablecoin settlement — added incrementally, not all at once.

Deployment & PHI residency

Private single-tenant, hybrid, or cloud, with data-residency controls and BAAs designed to support HIPAA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 requirements.

Every connection carries remittance and audit context end to end, so finance, IT, and compliance see one reconciled trail from claim submitted to cash settled — under your brand, on infrastructure you own.

What does ACM Global Tech provide for healthcare organizations?
