The challenge

Stop stitching together someone else's stack

Most fintechs assemble a patchwork of point vendors for accounts, payments, ledgers, and compliance, then spend years integrating and re-platforming as they scale.

That fragmentation slows launches, multiplies vendor risk, and leaves your roadmap hostage to providers who own the underlying infrastructure. When a sponsor bank changes terms or a processor deprecates an API, your product bears the cost.

ACM takes a different path. We deliver one cohesive ecosystem, engineered for regulated institutions, that you operate and brand. The infrastructure is yours, not rented through a black box you cannot inspect or extend.

What you get

A full banking ecosystem, white-labeled

Launch deposit, lending, payment, and asset products on a single, modern platform, with the components you need today and room for what comes next.

Core banking

A modern ledger for deposits and lending, ready to power checking, savings, and credit products under your brand.

Payments and wallets

Money movement and mobile wallets across rails, so customers can pay, send, and hold value inside your app.

Card issuing

Issue branded debit and credit cards programmatically, with controls and reporting built for scale.

Exchange and multi-currency FX

Real-time FX across 200+ markets with industry-leading rates, ready for cross-border and multi-currency products.

RWA tokenization

Tokenize real-world assets on compliance-ready rails, with non-custodial options where your model calls for it.

Stablecoin settlement

Settle and move value with stablecoin rails for faster, programmable, lower-friction transfers.

Why ACM

  • Post-quantum cryptography: forward-looking security so the data and value you handle stay protected as the threat landscape changes.
  • Client-owned and brandable: the full stack is yours to operate and present as your own, not a third-party experience bolted onto your app.
  • Agile Speed Framework: a delivery model focused on getting you to a working, launchable product quickly, then iterating.
  • AI and data innovation: intelligence built into the platform to support smarter operations, risk, and customer experiences.
  • Up to 95% lower infrastructure cost: a platform value proposition targeting dramatic savings versus legacy core stacks.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can we white-label and own the banking infrastructure?

Yes. ACM delivers a complete white-label banking ecosystem, from core banking and payments to FX, cards, and tokenization, that is client-owned and brandable. You operate and present the stack as your own product rather than embedding a third-party black box.

Which capabilities can a fintech launch with ACM?

Core banking for deposits and lending, payments and mobile wallets, card issuing, real-time exchange and multi-currency FX across 200+ markets, RWA tokenization with non-custodial options, and stablecoin settlement, all on one platform.

Is the platform built for regulated environments?

ACM is regulated-first and compliance-ready by design, engineered for the scrutiny that banks, credit unions, and their auditors apply, and secured with post-quantum cryptography. This helps fintechs meet the bar required by enterprise and regulated customers.

How quickly can we go live?

ACM uses an Agile Speed Framework focused on rapid delivery, getting you to a working, launchable product quickly and then iterating. Schedule a discovery call and we will outline a path to launch for your specific product.
