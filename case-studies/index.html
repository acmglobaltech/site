Scenarios

Representative modernization scenarios

Two scenarios are detailed below, with more in development. Each starts from a real institutional pressure, maps it to the ACM ecosystem, and ties target outcomes to cited benchmarks — so you can judge the approach on its merits, not on marketing claims.

Credit union core modernization

A relationship-first credit union replaces a legacy core with a cloud-native, client-owned platform — targeting faster digital banking, real-time payments, and lower operating cost, against ACM's up-to-95% lower infrastructure cost value proposition versus legacy core stacks. Read the scenario.

Healthcare payments & revenue cycle

A provider network deploys programmable payment rails and settlement to compress revenue-cycle friction and administrative work, set against the roughly $570B per year of US healthcare administrative waste reported across JAMA and Health Affairs. Read the scenario.

Cross-border FX for a corporate treasury

A mid-market corporate consolidates multi-currency settlement onto real-time FX across 200+ markets, targeting tighter spreads and faster value dates than fragmented legacy banking relationships. Scenario — coming soon.

Embedded finance for a fintech

A regulated fintech launches branded accounts, card issuing, and payments on a white-label, compliance-ready stack — modernizing incrementally instead of a rip-and-replace. Scenario — coming soon.

Broker-dealer RWA issuance

A broker-dealer stands up tokenized real-world asset issuance and settlement with non-custodial options, set against BCG's projection of $16T+ in tokenized RWA by 2030. Scenario — coming soon.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are these case studies based on named clients?

No. These are representative scenarios, not named-client results. ACM Global Tech does not yet publish named-client case studies, so each scenario is illustrative and grounded in public industry benchmarks rather than claimed actual outcomes for a specific institution.

Where do the benchmarks come from?

Scenario targets are tied to cited public references — for example BCG's projection of $16T+ in tokenized real-world assets by 2030, roughly $570B per year in US healthcare administrative waste reported across JAMA and Health Affairs, and ACM's platform value proposition of up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks.

Can ACM model a scenario for our institution?

Yes. During a discovery call we map your core, compliance posture, and goals to a regulated-first, client-owned solution, then outline target outcomes and a realistic delivery timeline specific to your institution.
