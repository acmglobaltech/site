The gap

Crime is real-time. Your controls are not.

Most compliance and risk still run on overnight batches and brittle, hand-tuned rules. Financial crime does not wait for the nightly job. By the time a flag lands the next morning, the money is gone and the analyst is buried in noise.

Batch lag

Decisions arrive too late

Overnight scoring catches yesterday's fraud. Real-time payments and instant rails demand a decision before the transaction clears.

Rules drift

Brittle, static logic

Hand-coded thresholds miss novel patterns and flood teams with false positives. Tuning never keeps pace with attackers.

Silos

Data trapped in pieces

Signals live in separate systems that never meet. Without a unified view, the model never sees the whole picture.

Black box

Hard to explain

Opaque scores stall investigations and rattle examiners. Every decision needs a reason an auditor can follow.
