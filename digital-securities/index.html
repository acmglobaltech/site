The context

Legacy rails were not built for this.

Matching, clearing, and the share register still run on decades-old, siloed systems: batch-bound, slow to settle, expensive to reconcile, and built on cryptography that quantum computers will break. Meanwhile tokenization is moving from pilot to production, and the infrastructure underneath has to keep up.

Batch

Settlement lag

T+1 and T+2 cycles tie up capital and add counterparty risk that real-time, atomic settlement removes.

Silos

Fractured records

Trading venue, broker books, and the transfer agent each keep a copy. Reconciliation is constant, costly, and error-prone.

RSA / ECC

Quantum-vulnerable

The cryptography securing today's securities systems falls to a quantum computer. Records meant to last decades are exposed now.

Tokenization

Production, not pilots

Tokenized securities are reaching live markets. The rails beneath them must be compliant, programmable, and ready.

What we deliver

Issuance to register, one platform.

The three pillars of a regulated securities market, modernized and tokenized, working as one system instead of three disconnected ones.

Alternative trading system

Real-time order matching with built-in market surveillance and regulatory reporting. The venue, modernized.

Broker-dealer

Investor onboarding, KYC/AML, books-and-records, and net-capital tracking, integrated end to end.

Transfer agent

A programmable share register: corporate actions, distributions, and ownership of record, maintained on-ledger.

ERC-1400 security tokens

Standards-based security tokens with transfer restrictions and compliance enforced at the protocol level.

Tokenized settlement

Atomic, real-time settlement on-ledger. Delivery and payment clear together, no batch window.

Non-custodial MPC custody

Threshold signing and multi-party computation secure assets with no single point of compromise.
