The Problem

Legacy lending slows growth and frustrates borrowers

Manual underwriting, batch processing, and disconnected point systems make lending slow, expensive, and inconsistent. Borrowers abandon applications, staff re-key data across tools, and risk teams lack a single view of every decision.

ACM replaces that patchwork with one cloud-native lending stack. We modernize auto, mortgage, consumer, and small-business lending on a platform you brand and own, so you can compete on speed and experience while keeping decisions explainable and audit-ready.

Capabilities

A complete digital lending lifecycle

Origination, decisioning, and servicing on a single platform, with AI applied where it improves accuracy and speed, and human oversight where it matters.

Digital origination

Branded online and mobile applications with document capture, pre-fill, and real-time status, designed to reduce abandonment and manual entry.

AI credit decisioning

Predictive scoring and configurable risk models that support faster, more consistent decisions, with clear, explainable reasoning behind each outcome.

Embedded fraud defense

Real-time fraud signals and identity checks at application, backed by post-quantum cryptography across the stack.

Decisioning

AI decisioning that is fast, fair, and defensible

Speed cannot come at the cost of compliance. ACM pairs predictive analytics with the controls regulated lenders need.

  • Explainable outcomes: every decision carries the factors behind it, supporting adverse-action requirements and internal review.
  • Configurable policy: your credit policy, thresholds, and overlays stay in your control, not buried in a black box.
  • Consistent treatment: automated scoring applies the same criteria to every applicant, reducing variability across loan officers.
  • Full audit trail: decisions, data, and model versions are logged end to end for examiners and risk teams.

Decisioning is powered by the same data foundation behind our wider platform. Learn more about our AI & data capabilities and how unified data turns siloed records into faster, sounder credit decisions.

Integration

Connects to the lending systems you already run

Modernizing origination should not mean re-platforming your bureau feeds, document store, or core ledger. ACM's lending stack exposes REST APIs and SDKs that sit over your existing systems of record, so loan data flows in and out without re-keying.

Core & ledger

Booked loans, balances, and payment events sync to your existing core or to the ACM banking core, keeping deposits, payments, and loan accounting reconciled on one ecosystem.

Bureau & KYC

Connectors pull credit-bureau pulls, income and identity verification, and KYC/AML signals into decisioning, with provider results normalized for every applicant.

Documents & eSign

Application packages, disclosures, and signed agreements move between origination, your document vault, and eSign tools, so audit copies stay attached to each loan file.

Where you keep a legacy loan-origination or servicing system, ACM runs alongside it and migrates products in stages rather than forcing a single cutover.

Migration & Rollout

A staged path from legacy lending to production

Our Agile Speed Framework stands up a working lending skeleton early, so origination, decisioning, and servicing run in production on one product before you expand across the portfolio.

  • Start with one product line: launch a single book, such as consumer or auto, in parallel with your legacy system to validate workflows on real applications.
  • Decisioning in shadow first: run AI scoring alongside current underwriting to compare outcomes before automated approvals carry live volume.
  • Migrate servicing in waves: move existing loans on connected payment and statement rails in controlled batches, with reconciliation at each step.
  • Governed by audit and roles: RBAC, SSO, and end-to-end logging of decisions, data, and model versions apply from day one, giving risk and examiners a clear trail through every stage.

The result is faster origination on infrastructure you brand and own, reached without a big-bang core replacement. Explore the Agile Speed Framework behind the rollout.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does lending modernization with ACM include?

ACM modernizes the full lending lifecycle on one white-label platform: digital origination, AI-driven credit decisioning, automated approvals, and loan servicing automation. It supports auto, mortgage, consumer, and small-business lending, and connects to deposits, payments, and treasury so you avoid stitching together separate point systems.

How does ACM keep AI lending decisions compliant?

Decisioning is built for regulated institutions. Every outcome is explainable with the factors behind it, your credit policy and thresholds stay configurable and in your control, and decisions, data, and model versions are logged end to end for audit. Automated scoring also applies consistent criteria to every applicant to reduce variability.

Can we keep our brand and own the lending platform?

Yes. ACM lending is fully white-label, so you brand and own the entire experience from application through servicing rather than renting a vendor's interface. The platform is cloud-native and targets up to 95% lower infrastructure cost versus legacy core stacks.

Do we have to replace our core banking system first?

No. ACM modernizes lending on a cloud-native stack that connects to your existing core, then runs alongside deposits, payments, and treasury within one ecosystem. Our Agile Speed Framework stands up a working lending skeleton early so you can validate workflows in production and iterate from there.
