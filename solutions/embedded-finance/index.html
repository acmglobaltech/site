What it is

One platform, your brand, embedded everywhere

Embedded finance means delivering banking where your members and customers already are — at checkout, inside a partner portal, or within your own mobile experience — without sending them to a separate institution.

ACM provides the regulated-first core that sits underneath: deposits and lending, real-time payments, mobile wallets, multi-currency FX, card issuing, and stablecoin settlement. You expose only the pieces you need, under your own brand and your own control. The infrastructure is white-label and client-owned, so the relationship, the data, and the user experience stay with you.

This is the same ecosystem that powers ACM's core banking, treasury, and tokenization capabilities — assembled into clean, composable services so you can ship a financial product in weeks rather than rebuild a bank from scratch.

Capabilities

The building blocks of an embedded program

Start with one capability or compose several. Each is brandable, API-accessible, and built for institutions that answer to regulators.

Accounts & deposits

Open, fund, and manage branded deposit and sub-account structures programmatically, with ledgering designed for institutional reconciliation.

Embedded payments

Move money in real time — account-to-account transfers, disbursements, and collections — across domestic rails and partner flows.

Card issuing

Issue physical and virtual cards on demand, set spend controls, and tie cards directly to the accounts you run on the platform.

Mobile wallets

Stand up branded wallets with stored value, tokenized card credentials, and in-app payment experiences your users keep coming back to.

Exchange & multi-currency FX

Hold, convert, and settle across 200+ markets with industry-leading exchange rates, embedded directly into your product flows.

Stablecoin settlement

Settle and reconcile with stablecoins for faster, lower-cost movement, with non-custodial options where your model calls for it.

RWA tokenization

Represent real-world assets on-chain and connect tokenized value to the same accounts, payments, and settlement layer.

Treasury & liquidity

Manage balances, sweeps, and liquidity across the programs you operate, with reporting built for finance and risk teams.

How ACM is different

Regulated-first, people-first, and quantum-ready

Embedded finance only works if it can survive the scrutiny of a bank's risk, compliance, and security teams. That is the bar we build to.

  • Compliance-ready by design: built for regulated institutions, with controls, audit trails, and reporting that hold up under examination.
  • Client-owned and brandable: the platform is white-label end to end, so your members and customers experience your brand, not ours.
  • Post-quantum cryptography: settlement and data protection engineered for the next era of cryptographic risk, not just today's.
  • Agile Speed Framework: a delivery method designed to move you from scoping to a live, branded program quickly and predictably.
  • AI and data innovation: applied where it earns its place — fraud signals, reconciliation, and operational insight — alongside ecosystem partners Hanzo.ai and Lux Finance.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is embedded finance?

Embedded finance is the delivery of banking services - accounts, payments, cards, and settlement - directly inside a non-bank product or partner experience, instead of routing users to a separate institution. ACM Global Tech provides the regulated-first, white-label infrastructure that sits underneath, so credit unions, banks, and the brands they serve can offer these services under their own brand and keep ownership of the customer relationship.

How is ACM's embedded finance different from typical banking-as-a-service?

The platform is client-owned and fully brandable rather than rented, so your institution retains the relationship, the data, and the experience. It is built regulated-first for examination by serious risk and compliance teams, uses post-quantum cryptography for settlement and data protection, and is delivered through ACM's Agile Speed Framework to move from scoping to a live program quickly.

Which capabilities can I embed?

You can compose accounts and deposits, embedded real-time payments, physical and virtual card issuing, mobile wallets, multi-currency FX across 200+ markets, stablecoin settlement with non-custodial options, real-world asset tokenization, and treasury and liquidity management. Start with a single capability or assemble several into one program.

Is ACM's embedded finance suitable for regulated institutions?

Yes. The platform is built for regulated institutions and is compliance-ready, with controls, audit trails, and reporting designed to hold up under examination. ACM positions itself as regulated-first and compliance-ready; specifics around any charter or regulatory standing are addressed directly with each institution.
