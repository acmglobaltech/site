Members expect more than a place to bank

Members compare their banking app to every other digital experience they use. Generic alerts, one-size-fits-all offers, and disconnected channels quietly erode the loyalty that community institutions are built on.

ACM's member engagement layer sits on top of your core, deposits, lending, and payments to make every touchpoint relevant. It is fully brandable and client-owned, so the relationship stays between your institution and your members, never with a third-party brand.

How It Works

Your data, your brand, your relationships

Engagement is only as good as the foundation beneath it. ACM unifies member data and applies it responsibly.

  • Unified member profile: Connect deposits, lending, payments, and wallet activity into a single view of each member.
  • AI and data innovation: Machine learning models translate that data into relevant, plain-language recommendations, powered by our AI & data capabilities.
  • White-label by default: Every screen, message, and notification carries your institution's brand, not ours.
  • Regulated-first design: Built for regulated institutions with governance, auditability, and consent baked in, not bolted on.
  • Post-quantum security: Member data is protected with post-quantum cryptography across the platform.

Integration & Interoperability

Fits the systems your members already touch

The engagement layer reads from the systems of record you already run and delivers to the channels members already use, so personalization reflects live balances and events rather than yesterday's export.

Core, deposits & lending feeds

Connectors and event hooks ingest account, transaction, and lifecycle data from your existing core, loan origination, and payments systems, in real time or batch.

CRM, KYC & consent sources

Sync member records, KYC status, and marketing-consent flags from your CRM and identity providers so journeys respect who can be contacted and how.

Open APIs & channel SDKs

REST and webhook APIs, plus mobile and web SDKs, let your teams trigger journeys, embed insights, and reconcile engagement events with downstream reporting.

Outbound delivery rails

Push, in-app, email, and SMS route through your existing providers or ours, with delivery and engagement status flowing back into the unified member profile.

Administration & Governance

Every message, consented and accountable

Outreach to members is regulated communication. The platform is built so marketing, retail, and compliance teams can run campaigns within clear roles, consent rules, and a complete record of what was sent.

  • Role-based access & SSO: Separate permissions for journey authors, approvers, and read-only analysts, federated through your existing SSO and identity provider.
  • Consent & preference enforcement: Opt-in, opt-out, and channel preferences are honored at send time, with suppression applied before any message leaves the platform.
  • Approval workflows: Route new journeys, offers, and templates through review and sign-off before they reach a single member.
  • Immutable audit logs: Capture who created, edited, approved, and triggered each journey, and which members received which message, for examiner and internal review.
  • Regulated-first & resilient: Governance, data residency options, and post-quantum cryptography are designed to support SOC 2, ISO 27001, and applicable privacy requirements.

The result is an engagement program your risk and compliance functions can stand behind, not a marketing tool bolted onto regulated member data.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is a member engagement platform?

It is the layer of banking technology that makes every member interaction relevant and personal, across mobile, web, and branch. ACM's platform unifies member data, then uses it to deliver tailored guidance, proactive insights, and coordinated messaging, all under your institution's own brand.

Is the platform white-label?

Yes. The member engagement platform is fully brandable and client-owned. Every screen, notification, and message carries your institution's identity, so the relationship stays between you and your members rather than with a third-party vendor brand.

Does it work with our existing core?

Yes. ACM's engagement layer is designed to sit on top of your core, deposits, lending, and payments. It is part of a complete, regulated-first banking ecosystem, so you can adopt it as one layer or alongside other ACM products.

How is member data protected?

ACM is built regulated-first, with governance, auditability, and consent designed in from the start. Member data is secured across the platform using post-quantum cryptography to stay resilient against current and emerging threats.
