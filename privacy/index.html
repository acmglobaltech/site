Privacy policy

This policy explains what information ACM Global Tech collects through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is a general summary and not legal advice.

What we collect

We collect only what you choose to send us. When you submit a contact or onboarding form, that typically includes your name, work email, phone, institution, your areas of interest, and any message you write. Our web host may also process standard server logs (such as IP address and user agent) to deliver and secure the site.

  • Form submissions: details you enter so we can respond to your inquiry and scope an engagement.
  • Operational logs: limited technical data used to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the site.
  • No tracking sold: this marketing site does not sell your information and does not use advertising trackers.

How we use it

  • To reply to your inquiry, schedule discovery calls, and scope onboarding.
  • To manage the relationship in our CRM and provide the materials you request.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website.

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us run the site and our CRM (for example, our hosting and customer-relationship tools), under appropriate confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

Your choices & contact

  • Access & deletion: email us to request a copy of, correct, or delete the information you've submitted.
  • Communications: you can ask us to stop contacting you at any time.
  • Security: we protect data with the controls described in our Trust & Security Center.

Questions or requests? Email info@acmglobaltech.com. We may update this policy from time to time; material changes will be posted on this page.
